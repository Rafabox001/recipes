***Lassagna recipe***
-Prepare the meat as you like (I prefer to combine it with mushrooms, tomatoe sauce, a little wine,
and also some bacon, you prepare it until everithing is well cook).
-Prepare bechamel sauce (http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/mario-batali/bechamel-sauce-recipe.html)
-Prepare a container to add to oven.
- Firt you shuld put a layer of pastry, put a thin layer of bechamel sauce, some of the meat, and
if you like cheese (i like it with 3 cheeses).
-Put in oven for 45-60 minutes.
-ENJOY!!
